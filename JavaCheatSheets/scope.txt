Scopes & Blocks

  Block
   - Are the range of where we can access our variables.
   - A block starts with an opening curly braces, and ends with a closing
     curly braces.

  Variable scope
   - Variable scope is the block/range of code that a variable can be  
     referred too.
   - A set of curly braces defines the bound of the scope.
   - Variables outside the scope cannot be access.
